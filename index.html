Peanut Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • Dried Peanuts
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Onions & Green Onions
  • Fresh parsley and basil
  • Black, White Pepper
  • Salt & Maggi
  • Onions
  • Fresh Fish/Meat
  • Oil

Procedure/preparation

  1. Fry Peanuts until dark brown and crunchy and let cool
  2. Blend peanut in coffee blender/blend in regular blender until very smooth
  3. Dice tomatoes, onions, and mince garlic and fry in vegetable oil
  4. Add meat{beef}, beef broth or water, black & white pepper, chopped basil, pepper, maggi & salt
  5. Let water boil, then add peanut blend and stir
  6. Let cook for about 10mins. If using fish, add fish at this time
  7. Add chopped parlsey and green oinions
  8. Taste for seasoning, and serve with Rice
